About

Hi! My name is Barbara Sanchez, I was born in Caracas, Venezuela. I am daughter of an Electrical Engineer and a Lawyer. I have three older sisters. My family gave me a loving home and a lot of values. I have great memories from my childhood, I was fortunate to being able to enjoy living in the countryside because I grew up in a city near the beach before moving at ten years old to the capital of my country Caracas.

As long as I can remember I enjoy using and learning how to use all types of technologies; so after high school I decided to study Computer Science, which I loved!. Then I change University after I moved to another city and Studied Psychology. I am married since 2012 to an amazing Man who is a Systems Engineer, and we have a beautiful 5 years old son name Santiago.
